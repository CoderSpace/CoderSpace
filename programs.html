Summer Apprenticeship

In our intensive 8-week apprenticeship youth learn three computer languages that power the web (HTML, CSS and JS), work in teams to build websites for local clients and showcase their work at Demo Day

Saturday Meet-up's

During the academic year CoderSpace hosts weekend meetups for students who are learning to code, leading clubs and working on client projects.

School Clubs

CoderSpace youth apply what they learned over the summer to lead coding clubs in their communities - at their school, local library or community center.
