Who We Are

CoderSpace creates engaging learning experiences to inspire a new generation of leaders and innovators. At CoderSpace, youth ages 14-17 learn to code, collaborate on local client projects and develop leadership skills for a digital world.

What We Do

We learn. CoderSpace youth participate in team-based, project-based learning experiences.

We bulid. CoderSpace youth work in teams to build websites for small businesses and community organizations.

We Lead. CoderSpace youth develop real-world experience in web development, teamwork and leadership.

How We Do It

CoderSpace is a community of practice powered by four core values:

Curiosity We ask questions and seek answers to deepen our understanding.

Creativity We solve problems with ingenuity and imagination.

Collaboration We work together and share our knowledge, skills and ideas.

Care We are thoughtful about what we do and how we treat each other.
