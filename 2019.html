2019 Summer Program

CoderSpace will not hold a summer program in 2019, as both co-founders have new job constraints. We'll spend this year working on staffing, curriculum, and building our board of directors so that the 2020 summer program will be our best yet.

We will continue our fall Saturdays starting in October, including a Hacktoberfest event and our annual holiday party. Please stay in touch by subscribing to our newsletter and we hope you'll join us in the fall.
