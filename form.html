Who We Are

CoderSpace creates engaging learning experiences to inspire a new generation of leaders and innovators. At CoderSpace, youth ages 14-17 learn to code, collaborate on local client projects and develop leadership skills for a digital world.

What We Do

We learn. CoderSpace youth participate in team-based, project-based learning experiences.We build. CoderSpace youth work in teams to build websites for small businesses and community organizations. We lead. CoderSpace youth develop real-world experience in web development, teamwork and leadership.

How We Do It

CoderSpace is powered by curiosity and collaboration. Investigation and exploration are central to our instructional approach. As a community of practice, we work together to share our knowledge, skills and ideas.
